about me

Hi, I am Tibbe. My biggest hobby is making music. The software I use for that is a DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) called LMMS, but in the future I might be using Ardour or Reaper.

the story

I started making music begin this year (2021) (for everyone in 2025 or later, corona was still a thing back then), with a free and open source daw called LMMS. I found out about that daw when I was attempting to make a shortfilm in Blender (wich failed) and i wanted to make the music myself. I didn't really know how to make music back then, but as I was still learning blender, the shortfilm failed, and thus I never tried making the music. I think a few months later I heard from a friend that he was making music, so I also started making music and my first song was born.
